Welcome to Worldbuilder.

The tool to help you create.

Running a tabletop campaign?

Worldbuilder can help you keep track of npcs, store maps and other important information, and make accessing your notes as easy as the click of a button.

Writing a book?

Worldbuilder can help you find inspiration, jot down notes for later, show off your work to your friends and even invite them to collaborate!

Going your own path?

Whether you're making a webcomic, creating a video game, or just looking to put something together for fun, Worldbuilder can help.

Worldbuilder is a tool designed to help you store and access information that can help you as you build out your project.

Why not start with the resources page, or go admire a friend's work?
