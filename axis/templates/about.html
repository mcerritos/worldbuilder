the tool

Worldbuilder is a tool for writers, dungeon masters, or anyone looking to keep track of a world of their own creation. A world is a wide, complicated thing to keep track of, and Worldbuilder allows for easy data storage, as well as providing questions and links to aspiring worldbuilders to help keep the inspiration flowing.

Worldbuilder was built using Javascript, Django, UIkit, Python, and Javascript. It runs on a PostgreSQL server.
